Overview

bNote is an PRC-20 token with staking. When you stake, your tokens are burned and you receive shares. When you end the stake, you mint back principal minus any penalties. Share rate can increase over time (HEX-like ratchet).

Staking

  • Min/Max days: On-chain enforced (shown in the app).
  • Longer Pays Better (LPB): Extra bonus based on years staked up to a cap.
  • Bigger Pays Better (BPB): Extra bonus based on stake size up to a cap.
  • APY: Displayed as APR (basis points) in the app.

Penalties

  • Early end: Scales with remaining days (up to the early penalty max).
  • Late end: Increases the longer you wait past unlock (up to the late penalty max).
  • Exact penalties are computed on-chain using block timestamps.

Share Rate

Share rate is used to convert bNote to staking shares. Ending high-performing stakes can ratchet the share rate upward, protecting earlier stakers (similar to HEX mechanics).

FAQs

Q: When do late penalties start?
A: Any time after the unlock day (end of your lock period).

Q: Do I have to keep the app open?
A: No. Staking state lives on-chain.
