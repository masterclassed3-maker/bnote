Quick Start
1) Connect
  • Open the app and click Connect Wallet.
  • Use a browser wallet (e.g., MetaMask) or a mobile wallet via WalletConnect.
  • Ensure you’re on PulseChain. Use “Add PulseChain” above if needed.
2) Stake
  • Enter Amount and Days (whole number).
  • Review the live Estimates: unlock date, LPB/BPB, shares, payout.
  • Click Stake and confirm the transaction.
3) End Stake
  • On or after the unlock day, end by the index in “Your Stakes” or the quick form.
  • Early/Late penalties may apply if not ended on the unlock day.
4) Export / Track
  • Use Export CSV or Copy CSV to track stakes.
  • Check the Analytics section for charts and market stats.

How Staking Works
  1. Stake start: You burn bNote and receive shares (not tradable). Share amount = your staked amount ÷ shareRate, then multiplied by LPB & BPB bonuses.
  2. While staked: Your stake accrues yield based on APR and protocol rules. Share rate is up-only and may increase globally when stakes end (see below).
  3. Stake end: You mint back principal + rewards (minus penalties if early/late). The end can also ratchet the global share rate up.
LPB & BPB Bonuses
LPB — Longer Pays Better
  • +20% shares per full 365 days staked.
  • Capped at 10 full years (maximum +200%).
Formula (bps): LPB = 2000 × min(10, floor(days/365))
BPB — Bigger Pays Better
  • Linear bonus based on amount, up to +10% at 210,000 bNote.
  • Amounts ≥ 210,000 bNote receive the full +10% BPB.
Formula (bps): BPB = 1000 × min(1, amount / 210,000) (all values in whole bNote)
Effective shares = (amount / shareRate) × (1 + (LPB+BPB)/10000)
Share Rate (Up-Only Ratchet)

Share rate determines how many shares you get per bNote. When large/long stakes end with strong performance, the protocol may raise the shareRate so that future stakes need more bNote to get the same shares. This protects early/long stakers and prevents dilution. The rate never goes down.

Early & Late Penalties
Early End
  • Ending before the full lock period applies a penalty that scales with the days remaining.
  • Maximum early penalty is 25% of principal.
Late End
  • Ending after the unlock day can apply a penalty that grows the longer you wait.
  • Maximum late penalty is 25%.

Tip: Ending on the unlock day avoids late penalties.

Worked Examples (Illustrative)
Example A — 10,000 bNote for 365 days
  • LPB = +20% (one full year). BPB ≈ +4.76% (10,000 / 210,000).
  • Total bonus ≈ 24.76% → effective shares ≈ (10,000 / shareRate) × 1.2476.
  • APR 3.69% (est. payout displayed in the app’s Estimates box).
Example B — 250,000 bNote for 1,095 days (~3y)
  • LPB = +60% (3 years). BPB hits cap = +10%.
  • Total bonus = +70% → effective shares ≈ (amount / shareRate) × 1.70.
  • Higher shares → stronger stake weight & yield path.

Note: These are simplified illustrations. Actual on-chain outcomes depend on timestamps and protocol math.

FAQ
Is the share rate supposed to go down?
No. The share rate is up-only. It may increase when stakes end so future stakes need more bNote to earn the same shares.
Do LPB/BPB bonuses increase my principal?
No. Bonuses increase your shares at stake start, which affects your stake’s weight and potential payout. Principal stays the same.
What happens if I end early or late?
A penalty may apply (up to 25%), scaling with time left (early) or time past unlock (late). Ending on the unlock day avoids late penalties.
Why are price stats missing sometimes?
Price/volume rely on third-party data. If no pair is detected or the API is down, those KPIs may be unavailable temporarily.
Are Estimates exact?
They’re guidance only. Actual results depend on on-chain timestamps, rate changes, and contract rules.
Troubleshooting
  • “Failed to switch networks” — Click Add PulseChain above, approve in your wallet, then reconnect.
  • “User rejected” — You canceled in wallet. Try again and confirm.
  • “Invalid stake days” — Enter a whole number between the min and max shown.
  • WalletConnect won’t pair — Ensure your domain is on the allowed list in WC Cloud, then retry the QR/connect flow.
Glossary
  • APR: Annual Percentage Rate (simple, not APY compounding).
  • LPB/BPB: Longer/Bigger Pays Better share bonuses.
  • Share Rate: Global conversion between bNote and shares (up-only).
  • Shares: Internal accounting units representing your stake weight.
  • Basis Points (bps): Hundredths of a percent (100 bps = 1%).
